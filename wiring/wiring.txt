4. Important Notes
GPIO34 and GPIO35 are ADC1 pins and work correctly with WiFi.
Do NOT use ADC2 pins while WiFi is enabled.
All GND connections must be common.
Adjust the ZMPT101B potentiometer during calibration.
Ensure SCT013 clamps ONLY around ONE live wire.
Never connect ESP32 directly to AC mains.
